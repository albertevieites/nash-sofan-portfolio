Project Scope

Magnett端 contracted Michel, Jan and I to collaborate with their CTO to design and architect a MVP. They needed a company marketing landing page, user dashboard, and manager dashboard to show investors to realize the initial seed funding in Spring 2022.

Understanding the problem

Businesses that are struggling to lower cost per leads due to the loss of efficiency from social media channels, need a way to increase lead conversions and build brand awareness from within the business.

Hypothesis

Creating an easy tool to help employees play a more active in social media and managers to track content, will generate lead conversions at a lower cost. Hence, increase company sales, decrease marketing spending, and empower employees to build a personal brand.
